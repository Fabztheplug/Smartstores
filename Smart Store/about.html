Who We Are

We pride ourselves on offering a completely flexible service, with a wide choice of storage units that can be fitted exactly to your specifications. Ranging from the size of a telephone kiosk (10 sq. ft./1 sq. m.), to the size of a tennis court (1,000 sq. ft./100 sq. m.), we offer a range of sizes to suit you. It’s not all just one size fits all, so get in touch with our team to find out how we can customise a unit for you.

Our focus is on delivering an unbeatable customer service through our simple, transparent approach to personal, business, and student storage. You’ll find a passionate team of local staff members on-hand during office hours to lend a hand wherever they’re needed. Simply ask our team how they can help!.
